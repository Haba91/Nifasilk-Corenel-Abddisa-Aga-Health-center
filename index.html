About Us

Mission: To provide accessible, quality primary healthcare services to the community, with a focus on prevention and control of common diseases, while also supporting broader health initiatives and education.

Target Population: 41,000 individuals

ተልዕኮ: በቀላሉ የሚደርስ እና የጥራት የመጀመሪያ ደረጃ የጤና አገልግሎት ማቅረብ፣ በተለይም በተደጋጋሚ በሚታዩ በሽታዎች መከላከል እና መቆጣጠር ላይ ትኩረት በማድረግ እና የጤና እርምጃዎችን እና ትምህርትን በደጋፊነት ማገዝ ነው።

Our Services

  • Emergency Services
  • Maternal and Child Health: Antenatal care, postnatal care, and child health services
  • Immunization: Routine vaccinations for children and adults
  • Management of Common Diseases: Treatment of respiratory infections, diarrhea, malaria
  • Family Planning: Contraceptive services and counseling
  • Reproductive Health Services
  • Health Education: Disease prevention, healthy lifestyle promotion
  • Disease Prevention and Control: Including HIV/AIDS, TB, and other communicable diseases
  • Referral Services: Referral to higher-level facilities for specialized care
  • Community Outreach: Community health programs and awareness
  • Health System Strengthening: Supporting basic healthcare infrastructure and referral networks
